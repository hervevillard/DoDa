# Privacy Policy for DoDa

*Last updated: June 10, 2026*

DoDa ("the App") is a children's learning app developed by Hervé Villard.

## Data We Collect

**We collect no data.** The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information about you or your child — not names, not device identifiers, not usage statistics, not anything.

## No Analytics or Advertising

The App contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising networks, and no third-party tracking of any kind.

## No Account Required

The App requires no account creation, login, or registration.

## Children's Privacy

The App is designed for young children. Because we collect no data, there is nothing to disclose, share, or delete under COPPA, GDPR-K, or similar regulations.

## Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our data practices, we will update this page and the "last updated" date above.
